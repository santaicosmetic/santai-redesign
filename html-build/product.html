Pitch is the Santai daily wear — engineered for the woman who walks into the boardroom and never has to think twice about her lashes. The lightweight magnetic band sits seamlessly along your lash line: no glue, no lifting at the corners, no irritation. Six micro-magnets embedded in a flexible polymer band deliver a defined, natural-finish look that holds for up to 16 hours of office-to-dinner wear.

Crafted from premium synthetic mink-effect fibres, Pitch suits almond, hooded, and double-lid eye shapes beautifully. Reusable up to 30 times when cared for properly — store flat in the included compact, wipe gently with the care brush, never touch with mascara. Hypoallergenic, contact-lens friendly, water-resistant against rain, mist, and steam.

The honest daily lash that doesn't tug, pull, or pretend. Made for working women in Kuala Lumpur and beyond.

Step 1. Apply Santai magnetic liner along your lash line. Let dry fully (~30 seconds).

Step 2. Hold the band a hair above your natural lashes. The magnets guide themselves home.

Step 3. Press gently from the inner corner outward to seat the band.

To remove: slide a fingertip along the band and lift cleanly. No tugging, no residue.
