Foam Cleanser.

ACC-CLN-001

The 60-second daily wash that keeps your magnetic lashes wearable for 30+ uses. No oils, no residue, no demagnetising.

Magnet-safe Quick-dry 12-month care
RM 29

Free shipping across Malaysia · Pairs with every lash style

Foam Cleanser is the dedicated daily wash for magnetic lash bands and the lid skin underneath. The micellar foam lifts oil, mascara residue, sunscreen, and sweat without disturbing the magnets — so your band stays calibrated and your lid stays clear. One pump, 60 seconds, no rinsing required.

Designed for daily use. Free of the glue solvents, alcohols, and silicones that degrade magnetic lash bands prematurely. Hypoallergenic, fragrance-free, and built to slot into the standard Santai routine: wear, cleanse, store, repeat.

100ml pump bottle — lasts about 3 months at daily use. Formulated and bottled in Selangor.

Klang Valley: same-day dispatch on orders placed before 2pm.

West Malaysia: 1–3 working days via J&T or Pos Laju.

East Malaysia: 3–5 working days.

Free shipping across Malaysia, every order.

International shipping available — see our shipping page for rates.

Step 1. After removing your magnetic lashes, place them on the included pad.

Step 2. Pump 1–2 doses of foam directly onto the band.

Step 3. Wait 60 seconds, then wipe with a clean microfibre or cotton round.

Step 4. Air-dry flat. Store in your Santai compact — not in the bathroom.
