Legal · Privacy

Privacy policy.

How Santai Cosmetics Sdn Bhd collects, stores, and uses your personal data, in line with Malaysia's Personal Data Protection Act 2010 (PDPA).

1. Who we are

Santai Cosmetics Sdn Bhd ("Santai", "we", "our", "us"), registered in Malaysia under company number 1234567-X, with our studio at Level 3, Eaton Mall, Jalan Damai, Bukit Bintang, 50200 Kuala Lumpur, is the data controller for personal information collected through santai-cosmetics.com.

2. What we collect

You give us directly

  • Name, email address, shipping address, phone number — when you place an order, create an account, or join the newsletter.
  • Payment details — processed by our payment partners (FPX, GrabPay, TnG, Boost, Atome, Stripe). We never store full card numbers.
  • Messages you send via the contact form, email, or WhatsApp.
  • Quiz responses — Lash Match answers, used to recommend a fit. Not tied to your identity unless you save the result to your account.

Collected automatically

  • Device, browser, and IP address — for site performance, fraud prevention, and analytics.
  • Pages visited, time on site, referral source — via cookies and analytics tools (see Section 6).

3. How we use it

  • To fulfil and ship your orders, issue refunds, and provide customer care.
  • To send order confirmations, shipping updates, and account-related emails.
  • With your consent, to send newsletters about new launches, restocks, and styling notes.
  • To improve the site, our products, and our service.
  • To comply with Malaysian law, including tax, accounting, and consumer-protection obligations.
  • To prevent fraud and protect against abuse.

4. Who we share it with

We never sell your data. We share it only with vendors who help us run Santai, and only with the minimum data they need:

  • Shopify Inc. — our e-commerce platform.
  • J&T, Pos Laju, DHL — shipping carriers (name, address, phone).
  • FPX / GrabPay / TnG / Boost / Atome / Stripe — payment processors.
  • Klaviyo — email and SMS for order updates + newsletter (only if you've subscribed).
  • Judge.me — review collection (email used to invite a review post-purchase).
  • Google Analytics — anonymised site analytics.

If we ever onboard a new vendor that processes personal data, this list gets updated.

5. How long we keep it

  • Account & order data — for as long as you have an account, plus 7 years after closure (Malaysian tax requirements).
  • Newsletter subscriptions — until you unsubscribe.
  • Contact form messages — 2 years after resolution.
  • Lash Match quiz results (anonymous) — 12 months.
  • Analytics data — 26 months (Google Analytics default).

6. Cookies

We use cookies for three things: keeping you logged in, remembering your cart, and understanding how the site is used. You can disable cookies in your browser, but the cart and login won't work without them.

Third-party cookies on our site come from Shopify (essential), Google Analytics (analytics), and Klaviyo (only if you've engaged with our emails).

7. Your rights under PDPA 2010

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correct any inaccurate or outdated data.
  • Withdraw consent for marketing communications at any time (one-click unsubscribe in every email).
  • Request deletion of your data (subject to the 7-year tax retention requirement above).
  • Lodge a complaint with the Personal Data Protection Department of Malaysia.

To exercise any of these, email privacy@santai-cosmetics.com. We respond within 21 calendar days as required by the PDPA.

8. Children

Santai products are not marketed to anyone under 16. We don't knowingly collect data from anyone under 16. If you believe we have, please contact us and we'll delete it.

9. International transfers

Some of our vendors (Shopify, Klaviyo, Google) process data outside Malaysia, primarily in Singapore, the EU, and the US. They are bound by their own data-protection commitments, which we vet before onboarding.

10. Changes to this policy

We'll update this page when our practices change. The "last updated" date below always reflects the current version. Material changes that affect existing customers will trigger an email notice.

Questions about anything on this page? Email privacy@santai-cosmetics.com or use the contact form.
