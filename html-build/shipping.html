Shipping

Free across Malaysia.

Every Santai order ships free within Malaysia, East and West. No minimum spend. International rates and timing below.

Free shipping across all of Malaysia — every order, no minimum.

Klang Valley orders placed before 2pm dispatch the same working day.

Dispatch times

Orders placed before 2pm MYT on a working day are picked, packed, and dispatched the same day. Orders placed after 2pm, on weekends, or on public holidays dispatch the next working day.

You'll receive a tracking link by email and SMS the moment your parcel leaves the studio.

International shipping

We ship to most of Southeast Asia, Australia, the UK, and the EU. Rates are calculated at checkout based on weight and destination.

Duties and taxes for international orders are calculated at checkout. We use Delivered Duty Paid (DDP) where possible — no surprise customs fees at your door.

Tracking your order

Once your parcel is collected, you'll get a tracking link by both email and SMS. You can also check status from your account dashboard at any time.

If your tracking hasn't updated for 48 hours, please get in touch and we'll chase the carrier on your behalf.

Missing or damaged parcels

Every Santai parcel is photographed at the studio before it leaves us. If your order arrives damaged or doesn't arrive at all, contact us within 7 days of the expected delivery date and we'll either resend or refund — your choice.
