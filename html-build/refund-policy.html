Refund policy.

The legal framing of how refunds work at Santai. For the friendlier walk-through, see our returns page.

This policy describes refund rights under Santai's commercial terms and applicable Malaysian law (Consumer Protection Act 1999).

1. Eligibility window

Refund requests must be submitted within 14 calendar days from the date the product was delivered to the address on the order.

Where a defect is not reasonably discoverable within 14 days (e.g. latent manufacturing defect), the statutory right to redress under the Consumer Protection Act 1999 remains available beyond this window.

2. Products eligible for refund

  • Unopened, unused products in original packaging.
  • Products that have been opened and tried once where the customer determines the fit is unsuitable (Santai's discretionary "try-first" allowance).
  • Products with manufacturing defects, regardless of use stage.
  • Products damaged in transit, where damage is reported within 7 days of delivery.
  • Products materially different from the description or images shown at point of sale.

3. Products not eligible for refund

  • Magnetic eyelashes used more than once (hygiene exception).
  • Foam Cleanser bottles where the seal has been broken or the product has been dispensed.
  • Thermo Curler units with cosmetic damage or charging-port damage attributable to misuse.
  • Items purchased on final-sale promotions, where this restriction was clearly disclosed at point of sale.
  • Items purchased through third-party stockists, which must be returned to the stockist directly.

4. Refund process

  1. Submit a refund request via hello@santai-cosmetics.com or the contact form, including order number and reason.
  2. Santai will respond within one working day with a prepaid return label (for Malaysian orders) or international return instructions.
  3. Pack the item securely and ship within 7 days of receiving the return label.
  4. On receipt, Santai inspects the return within 3 working days.
  5. Approved refunds are issued to the original payment method within a further 3 working days.

5. Refund timing by payment method

Where the bank or wallet provider's processing time exceeds the above, Santai is not responsible for the additional delay.

6. Refund amount

Refunds are issued for the full product price paid by the customer. For Malaysian orders, original shipping is refunded if the order is being refunded in its entirety (since shipping is included in the order total). For partial refunds, original shipping is retained.

For international orders, original shipping charges are non-refundable unless the return is due to a Santai error (e.g. wrong item shipped, defective product).

7. Free-gift handling

Where an order qualified for a free gift (Foam Cleanser at 2 lashes, Thermo Curler at 3 lashes) and is being refunded:

  • If the gift is returned with the order, no further deduction.
  • If the gift is not returned and the qualifying lash count drops below the gift threshold after refund, Santai may deduct the gift's retail value (RM 29 for Cleanser, RM 39 for Curler) from the refund amount.
  • Defective lashes that are exchanged on warranty do not affect free-gift eligibility.

8. Cancellation before dispatch

Orders not yet dispatched may be cancelled with a full refund (including shipping) by contacting us as soon as possible. Once dispatched, the return process above applies.

9. Statutory rights

Nothing in this policy limits the consumer's statutory rights under Malaysian law, including the Consumer Protection Act 1999, the Sale of Goods Act 1957, or any other applicable legislation.

For the customer-facing walk-through of how to actually get a refund (with step-by-step instructions), see returns & exchanges.
