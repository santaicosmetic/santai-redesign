How to apply

Easier than mascara.

Thirty seconds, no glue, no commitment. Press play.

01

Place at the lash line

Hold the band a hair above your natural lashes. The magnets guide themselves home — you don't have to aim.

02

Press from the inner corner

Pinch gently inward, then outward. The strip seats itself along the lash line in one motion.

03

That's it

No glue. No drying. No commitment. Lift off cleanly at the end of the night.

Common mistakes

Honest fixes.

Mistake

Strip lifting at the corner.

Fix

You placed it too low. Lift, raise 1mm, the magnets re-seat.

Mistake

Looks too dramatic in daylight.

Fix

Try Inbox or Minutes — same band, lighter fibre.

Mistake

Gap between band and lash line.

Fix

Apply your magnetic liner first, fully dry, then the lash.

Mistake

Hard to remove.

Fix

Slide a fingertip along the band. They lift off in one piece.

First-timer checklist

Five minutes. A mirror at eye level.

  • 01Take five minutes — the first time only. After that, thirty seconds.
  • 02Mirror at eye level. Looking down hides the lash line.
  • 03Apply magnetic liner first. Let it dry fully before the lash.
  • 04Press from the inside corner outward, never the middle first.
  • 05If it doesn't sit right, lift and replace. The magnets are forgiving.
