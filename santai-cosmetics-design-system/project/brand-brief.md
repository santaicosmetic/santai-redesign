# Santai Cosmetics — Design System (v1.0, original brief)

> **Brand:** Santai Cosmetics — Magnetic Eyelashes, Made for Effortless Beauty
> **Origin:** Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia
> **Audience:** Modern Southeast Asian women (20–40), beauty-curious but time-poor; first-time lash users intimidated by glue-on lashes; existing lash wearers tired of mess and irritation.
> **Tagline candidates:** *"Lashes in a Snap."* / *"Effortless Eyes, Every Day."* / *"Santai — beautiful, the easy way."*

## 1. Brand Strategy & Tone

### 1.1 Brand essence
*"Santai"* means *"relaxed"* in Bahasa Malaysia / Indonesian. Santai Cosmetics is the antidote to the high-effort, glue-stained, irritating world of traditional lash extensions. The brand stands for **effortless luxury** — magnetic lashes that look editorial but require zero artistry, no glue, no mess, no commitment.

The aesthetic borrows the *modern editorial-luxury minimalism* of high-end clean-beauty brands (think weighty matte packaging, generous negative space, considered typography) and grounds it with **warm tropical neutrals** and a **distinctly Asian-modern softness** — never cold, never clinical.

### 1.2 Brand voice
- **Confident but warm.** "You've got this. Two clicks and you're done."
- **Editorial, not chatty.** Short sentences. Considered word choice. No emoji-heavy bubble-tea-girl voice.
- **Honest and reassuring.** Address the fear directly: *"First time? We've got you."*
- **Never pushy or salesy.** Information first, sale second.

### 1.3 Two pillar promises
1. **"The right lash for YOUR eye."** Solve choice paralysis.
2. **"Easier than mascara."** Solve the trust block.

Every page must serve at least one of these two pillars.
